Who am i?

I grew up in a small town on the shoreline of Connecticut known as Old Saybrook. After playing a ton of different sports, graduating, and meeting some of the best friends of my life I shipped out to Massachusetts Maritime Academy. The regimented lifestyle was different but I learned invaluable life lessons, sailed around South America, and played collegiate lacrosse. Nevertheless, I ended up transferring back to CT to go to UConn. Once the battle of getting into the School of Engineering succeeded my friends and I won UConn Senior design with Hydra and successfully graduated college with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Shortly after that I moved down to RTP in North Carolina where I met some really awesome people, explored the blue mountains, and the ever familiar east coast shoreline. The water is a lot nicer in the Atlantic than up in the shoreline and the stars seemed brighter. A lot of weird nondeterministic events occured for me to end up where I am today, but I couldn't be happier. I'm currently back up in New England working in Providence. Life is always busy, but I know where home is and I look forward to every day.
